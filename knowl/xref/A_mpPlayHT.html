Activity 3.4.6. When to play H, when to play T.

For any particular mixed (or pure) strategy of Player 2, we could find \(E_1(T)\) and \(E_1(H)\text{.}\)

(a)

Explain why if \(E_1(H) > E_1(T)\text{,}\) Player 1 should always play H.

(b)

Similarly, explain why if \(E_1(H) \lt E_1(T)\text{,}\) Player 1 should always play T.
