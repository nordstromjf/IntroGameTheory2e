Dilemmas such as Prisoner’s Dilamma and Volunteer’s Dilemma are used in some game shows and television competitions. For example, In Friend or Foe (2002-2003) after working together to win a pot of money, the contestants must decide if they are a “Friend” or a “Foe”. If they both pick Friend, they split the money. If one picks Foe, while the other picks Friend, Foe gets all the money. If they both pick Foe, they both get nothing.
